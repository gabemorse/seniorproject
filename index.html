Who knew knobs were so hard?

January 23

Today I worked on creating an oven from scratch. While I initially thought this would be a reasonably quick project, this quickly turned into a much more complicated endeavor. My biggest struggle was a tiny detail, adding flattened cones to the tops of the knobs. I eventually turned to the sketchup help center, from which I was able to infer the information I needed. This was important for several reasons. One, I learned a new skill, but more importantly I discovered a valuable resource I can use in the future when I encounter problems. Next week, I plan to complete the top of my stove. This includes burners and racks. I believe this will go faster as I am not working inside the model itself.
